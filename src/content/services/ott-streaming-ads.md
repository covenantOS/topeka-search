## What OTT and Streaming TV ads look like in Topeka

Walk through any neighborhood in Topeka and the TV in the front window is almost certainly connected to the internet. Roku, Fire TV, Hulu, YouTube TV, Tubi, the apps have replaced the cable box for most households. OTT advertising (over-the-top) puts your business inside those streams. A 15 or 30 second spot plays before or during the show someone is already watching in College Hill, NOTO, or out past SW Topeka.

The reason this matters for a local business is targeting. Broadcast and cable spray your ad across the entire Topeka DMA whether the viewer is a buyer or not. Streaming lets you aim. We can serve your spot to households in specific ZIP codes, in a certain income band, with kids, who own their home, who recently searched for what you sell. The screen is big, the ad plays full attention, and the budget goes to people who can actually become customers.

This is television-grade visibility priced like digital. You get the trust that comes from being on the same screen as the local news and the trust that comes from being on the same screen as someone's favorite show, without writing a check the size of a cable contract.
