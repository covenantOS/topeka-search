**Keyword research for the Topeka market.** We find the terms your customers type when they search on Bing, Yahoo, and DuckDuckGo, and we filter out the junk traffic that drains budgets.

**Geo-targeting and scheduling.** Your ads show to people in your service area, whether that is all of Shawnee County or a tighter radius around your shop. We set day and time scheduling so you are not paying for clicks at 3 a.m. when nobody answers the phone.

**Conversion tracking.** We set up tracking so you can see which clicks turn into calls and form fills. Without it, you are guessing. With it, every decision is backed by data.
