## What PPC management looks like in Topeka

Paid search puts your business at the top of Google and Bing the moment a Topeka customer searches for what you sell. That's the whole appeal. Someone in College Hill needs a service today, types it into their phone, and the first few results get the clicks. PPC management is the work behind those results: the keyword research, the ad copy, the bidding, the geo-targeting, and the constant trimming of spend that doesn't pay off.

Local paid search is its own animal. A national campaign can afford to waste money learning. A Topeka business can't. We target the actual ZIP codes and neighborhoods you serve, from Downtown Topeka and NOTO to the edges of Shawnee County, so you're not paying for clicks from Kansas City or Wichita. We write ads that speak to local intent, because "tree removal in Topeka" and "tree removal" are two very different searches with two very different price tags.

The goal is high-intent clicks. Not impressions, not vanity traffic. People who are ready to call, fill out the form, or walk in the door.

## What's included

**Google Ads management.** Search campaigns built and run inside your own account, with keyword research, ad copy, geo-targeting, and weekly optimization.

**Microsoft Advertising (Bing).** A second channel that often delivers cheaper clicks in markets like Topeka and reaches buyers who never left Bing. We manage it alongside Google at no extra fee.

**Conversion tracking.** We wire up call tracking and form tracking so every dollar maps to a real lead, not a guess. You'll know your cost per lead, not just your cost per click.

**Negative keyword management.** We cut the searches that drain budget, the "free," the "jobs," the "DIY," so your money chases buyers instead of browsers.

**Ongoing copy testing.** New ad variations every cycle. The winners get more budget, the losers get retired.

**Monthly reporting.** Spend, leads, and cost per lead in language a busy owner can actually use.
