## What's included

**llms.txt setup.** We publish and maintain the file that tells AI crawlers what your business is, what you offer, and which pages to trust. It is the front door for the models, and most Topeka sites do not have one.

**Entity building.** We define your business as a clear entity across your site, your Google Business Profile, and the web, so an AI knows the "Topeka deck builder" it is reading about is unmistakably you and not a vendor three towns over.

**Citability rewrites.** We restructure your key pages into direct, quotable passages. AI engines lift clean answers, so we write the answer first and back it with local proof a model can cite.

**Schema markup.** We add and validate the structured data (LocalBusiness, Service, FAQ, and more) that hands the engines machine-readable facts about your hours, service area, and offerings.

**AI mention tracking.** Every month we check what Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, and Perplexity say when prompted with your real buying terms, and we report where you appear.

**Source authority work.** We strengthen the signals (reviews, references, consistent NAP data) that make an AI treat your business as a credible source worth quoting.
