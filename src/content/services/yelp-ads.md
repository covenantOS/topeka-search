## What Yelp Ads management looks like in Topeka

Yelp works differently than search. People land on Yelp already in buying mode. They are reading reviews, comparing two or three local providers, and getting ready to pick one. For a Topeka service business, that is a warm audience. A homeowner in NOTO or College Hill who pulls up Yelp to find a contractor is much closer to a decision than someone idly Googling at midnight.

The catch is that Yelp will happily spend your money whether or not the clicks make sense. Left on autopilot, ads show up for loosely related categories and pull clicks from outside your real service area. You pay for all of it. Active management is the difference between Yelp as a lead source and Yelp as a monthly charge you resent.

We run it as two jobs. First, the profile. A polished, complete Yelp page converts paid clicks into calls. Second, the spend. We manage the daily budget, the targeting, and the placements so your dollars stay pointed at buyers across the Topeka DMA who are actually likely to hire you.

## What's included

**Profile optimization first.** We rebuild the Yelp page before any ad budget goes live: correct categories, a description that sounds like your business, current hours, strong photos, and a call to action that gets people to pick up the phone.

**Budget protection.** We set and watch your daily spend so Yelp does not blow through your budget on weak clicks. Your management fee stays flat no matter what your budget is, so we never have a reason to inflate it.

**Tight local targeting.** We keep your ads focused on Topeka and the surrounding Shawnee County area instead of bleeding clicks to people you can't serve.

**Lead tracking.** We connect spend to outcomes so you can see which calls and messages came from Yelp, not just how many clicks you bought.

**Ongoing adjustments.** Placements get paused, categories get refined, and budget gets shifted toward what works. This is hands-on management, not set-and-forget.
