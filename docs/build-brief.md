## 0. Operating assumptions (read first)

1. **Zero start.** topekasearch.com is a fresh domain. It inherits nothing from servicelinepro.com. Every referring domain is earned from scratch. One internal link from servicelinepro.com is optional and minor, not load-bearing.
2. **Google is the only channel we buy for ourselves.** $1,000/mo, Topeka + DMA. No Bing, no ChatGPT ads, no Apple Maps, no Yelp, no LSA in our own spend.
3. **Every other platform is a service we sell and manage for clients.** Bing/Microsoft, ChatGPT ads, Apple Maps ads, Yelp ads, LSA, SEO, AI SEO, web dev, OTT/streaming, branding. Each gets its own page on the site.
4. **The pack is the prize.** Local search volume is thin. The map pack, driven by GBP, reviews, proximity, and categories, is where the few buyers click. Win it first.

## 4. Link plan (build from zero)

No big campaign needed. A clean 30 to 50 legitimate links clears the field. Priority order:

1. **Client footer credits.** "Website by Topeka SEO & PPC" on every site you build and host. Free, local, compounding. This is how Sprout got most of its links. Start with the two Topeka clients.
2. **Local civic and press:** Greater Topeka Partnership / GO Topeka (chamber + directory), Downtown Topeka Inc, Topeka Capital-Journal (cjonline) for launch/hire news, BBB Kansas.
3. **Agency directories that already rank for "topeka" terms:** Clutch, UpCity, DesignRush, The Manifest, Expertise.com (Topeka category), ThreeBestRated (Topeka). Get listed on all.
4. **Platform citations:** Google Business Profile, Apple Business Connect, Bing Places, Yelp, Thumbtack, LinkedIn Services. Keep NAP identical across every one.

## 5. Paid media — what we buy for ourselves

**One Google Search campaign. Topeka + DMA. $1,000/mo. Nothing else.**

The market is thin enough that $1k takes impression share on the money terms outright.

- **Campaign:** Search, geo = Topeka + Topeka DMA, location set to "presence: people in or regularly in your targeted locations."
- **Ad groups mapped one-to-one to landing pages:**
  - SEO → seo company, seo services, seo agency, seo topeka, local seo topeka
  - PPC → ppc management, google ads management, ppc agency, ppc topeka
  - Web design → web design, website design, web designer, web design topeka
  - Agency → marketing agency, digital marketing agency, advertising agency (+ topeka)
- **Match types:** phrase + exact to start. Add broad only if you want to deploy the full budget.
- **Negatives:** jobs, salary, free, course, classes, how to, internship, intern, diy, template, wordpress tutorial.
- **Conversions:** call tracking (calls over 60s), form submit, click-to-text. Bid to Maximize Conversions, switch to tCPA once you have 15 to 20 conversions.
- **Assets:** call asset, sitelinks (per service), callouts (local, owner-led, the 785), location asset tied to GBP.
- **Budget logic:** pure high-intent search may not spend the full $1k given the volume. To deploy all of it, add a Performance Max or Demand Gen layer for lead-gen across Topeka + DMA. To stay lean, let it underspend and own only the money terms. Your call, you run this for a living.

LSA is not available to us. Marketing and advertising agencies are not an eligible Local Services Ads category. LSA stays on the sell side only.
